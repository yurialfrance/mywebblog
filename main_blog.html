My Favorite K-Drama



Start Up has become one of my favorite k-dramas because It tells the story of young people who dream of success and who start working in start-ups in the Korean Silicon Valley. It tells their 'START' and their growth 'UP'. One of the characters here is Seo Dal Mi played by Bae Suzy. Seo DalMi entered the university however she left school because she wanted to earn more than one hundred million won to start a business. She has done so many part-time jobs. She started her social life early so she has connections and good relationships. Dal Mi's partner here is Nam Do San. Nam DoSan is the founder of SamSanTech. He loves coding and knitting. He likes to boast the time when he won the Youngest Grand Prize Math Olympiad as a child. However, 15 years later, his family is on the verge of humiliation. With his friends, he jumped onto a start-up business and it has been 2 years since the foundation but he has only been eating up the investment.
